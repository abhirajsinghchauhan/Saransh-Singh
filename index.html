Actor, Musician, Writer, Aspiring Producer

ACHIEVEMENTS


I've been part of this industry for 5 years and I've done serials like MERE SAI, GANESHA as a continuity lead. In my free time, my life is all about theatre, I've performed in plays like Jaliyawala bagh, DASPAT, Musibat mayke gai, Bichchoo, LGBTQ etc.

Apart from my present achievements, I've been entitled with many titles and won many contests. I was entitled with Mr. Chhattisgarh in 2011 followed by Mr. People's Choice and Mr. Ojas in 2014.

GET TO KNOW ME

I'm from the centre of India, Chhattisgarh. I've completed my graduation BE from RCET Bhilai. And after looking for job in IT in Bengaluru, I just knew, it is not my cup of tea. Hopefully I convinced my loving parents, and came to the city of dreams, Mumbai, to become a greatest actor. And I knew, it was my life's biggest decision and also felt the big responsibility. I immediately joined the theatre group to learn and observe good acting. And I got my first big break in serial MERE SAI Sony tv as continuity lead, the experience of shooting in front of a camera was awesome. And today I can say, I've fallen in love with this craft. And I've a long list of hobbies. I love reading manga, I'm crazy about anime, I can't live without shopping, Dancing and tracking and hiking excites me, video games, reading books, playing guitar, singing, composing songs, writing, painting, cooking etc. And My friends would describe me as an Entertainer, easy going, a great host, and a cook extraordinaire. Mumbai is the city of dreams and I've to start my own dream ,but I don't know how to get started . That's where Mystic act theatre and workshop group helped me to take my first step in my journey. There I learned the meaning of meditation and discipline in my life. Amano sir, as a mentor, told me the different aspects of being an actor and responsibilities comes with it. This is the place I explored myself and touched my true potential. Having a big dream may burden your shoulders, but Mystic acts shared it with me. And I'm glad to say, they are part of my family now. I've been part of this industry for 5 years and I've done serials like MERE SAI, GANESHA as a continuity lead. In my free time, my life is all about theatre, I've performed in plays like Jaliyawala bagh, DASPAT, Musibat mayke gai, Bichchoo, LGBTQ etc. Currently I'm working under Ashok bhatia sir & Lalit parimo sir. If I've to describe myself in one word , then that word would be an ENTERTAINER. I love to do it all. And I think there's nothing I can't do. My heart always pumps with the fuel called passion and I've deep respect for all art and artists. I don't think we all are different from each other, we'll have our own hurdles and our own way to overcome them. So I'm not judging anybody. The things that make me different from others , that would be my aloof creativity, imagination to see the world with lots of perspective and shaking hands with the hurdles. And I never ever go unnoticed, I just love to win hearts. I've been credited for my work in my state Chhattisgarh. I was shooting that time, when I came to know that there's a big hoarding all over the state and when I visited my hometown, they applauded my performance. I'm really grateful to all the love they have shown. We all artists share the responsibility of shaping the future. If we can change even one person's mentality with our work, then it's fruitful , though I want to live in a billion heart.
